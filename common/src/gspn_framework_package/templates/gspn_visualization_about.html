This module is used for the offline visualization of a GSPN simulation.

You can drag the places and the transitions to any place you want.

You can also zoom in the network and move it around.

On the left side you have the built GSPN. On the right side you have several metrics that you can test on the built GSPN. All you need to do is insert the input and click on the button on the right. The Result will appear inside the white box in the middle.
